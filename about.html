About Us

Welcome to our Image Watermarking Tool! We are a team of passionate developers dedicated to providing a simple and effective solution for watermarking your images with ease.

Our tool allows you to add watermarks to your images by selecting an image file, entering the desired watermark text, and choosing an encryption technique to secure your watermarked images.

Feel free to explore the different encryption options we provide, including MD5, SHA256, and Base64 encryption. We aim to make the process straightforward and user-friendly, ensuring that you can protect your images while maintaining their visual appeal.
